## Fragments of Etyo
The flayed and mangled pieces of the Aspect [[World/Aspects/Etyo]], strewn across the universe by [[Aurel, The Comet of Divine Indomination]] during revenge of her [[Stolen Legacy]].
They are intangible to mortals and require great power, typically unachievable by mortals, to harness. The deities are able to meaningfully interact with these shards but doing so is a violation of [[Etyan, Primordial of Etya]]'s laws and they will face his punishment.
### Shards of Etyo
The Shards of Etyo, Also Known as [[Laws Of Magic#Grails|Grails]], Are minor fragments of Etyo. Through the power of Etyan, these grails can nest themselves within mortals to act as a catalyst to manipulate the Etyo around them
# Malaiza
Owner everything in the universe. Everything that has, will or does exists belongs to it and everything that is not actively pulled from it will eventually return to it.
# Ilusioq
The aspect exists solely to spite Malaiza, it is the glove that allows the universe to reach into Malaiza, pilfer it's belongings and seize them for itself.
# Creation
Creation is not a dead, but it is no longer a god, rather it has become an underlying force. An instinct driven concept forever harvesting the scraps created from the eternal battle between Ilusioq and Malaiza.
