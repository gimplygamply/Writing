# The Greater Beings
`chronos *[0] The Greater Beings`
Those responsible for the previous universes collapse.
These deities, unable to accept the civilizations of their universe had surpassed their power, sought to destroy their universe and begin anew in one were they would neuter the power of it's inhabitants.
In the final moments of this universe, the gods were ambushed. Massacred by the civilizations they were fleeing. 
When the annihilation occurred, no great deity witnessed it. Their corpses became the cosmic jelly that the new universe would be formed from.
# The Lesser beings
`chronos *[0] The Lesser Beings`
These were advanced civilizations, lesser deities and natural phenomena.
Either through research, blind luck, divinity or inexplicable abnormalities, these entities were aware of their impending doom and that a new universe would be born thereafter.
