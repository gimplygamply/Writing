`chronos *[0] The Founding Forces`
# Elozor, The Root (Life)
a deity known for it's resilience that created an illusion of immortality, the burn shadows left behind from it's destruction are it's formless aspects and form the foundational concepts of this universe.
# Lyrdia, The Miser (Unknown)
a conceptually immortal and greedy deity. Their death violates the very nature of their existence.
# Nilatia, The Altruist (Known)
a deity the embodies spite and hatred. The fundamental enemy of "Unknown". It is a rabid dog whose sole purpose is to rip and tear from "unknown" and scatter it's pieces across the universe.
# Tesaset, The Ignorant (Creation)
A determined deity, ignorant and happy. It knows everything, aware of every single creation, concept and existence but with no mind or desire to utilize that power
