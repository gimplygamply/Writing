# 2. Select ONE fundamental trait
**ONLY ONE** key trait that summarizes them
- Smart
- Insecure
- Silly
# 3. Connect their fundamental trait to their physical traits
- Smart
	- Gives them Confidence so doesn't slouch
	- Causes them to be nerdy so keeps their head down
- Insecure
	- They feel like they need to crosses their arms
	- They always wears oversized clothing
- Silly
	- They don't mind having a loud voice
	- They smile often and their clothes are dirty/improper
