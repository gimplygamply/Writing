The devil is in the finer things, they don't need 18 rituals to get a point across only a few. And some stuff that is only referenced in text doesn't need it's whole entire page just for a reference.
Cultures aren't 'Built' They're 'grown'
# Food and Drink
- What do they eat?
- How do the prepare it?
- Ritual behind a shared meal?
# Greetings and Farewells
- Unique phrases/gestures
- Social rules about who speaks when
# Clothing with Meaning
- What is theres:
	- Everyday wear?
	- Festival wear?
		- Includes ritual wear
	- Symbols of Rank?
- Should reflect climate, trade and tradition
# Holidays
- Create a unique festival
- Coming of age traditions?
- Day of remembrance
- Celebrations, taboos and ceremonies
# Slang and Sayings
- What's the local jokes?
- How do people curse?
Go light on this one but apply granularly (each town has it's own smaller quirks on top of the over-arching ones)
# Measurements of time
- How do they measure time?
	- Seasonally?
	- Solar?
	- Moon phases?
	- Reigns of Kings?
Useful in revealing what matters to them. I.e. a seasonal culture likely has a strong agricultural root, whereas a reign of king culture likely is loyal or patriotic.
# Small Signs of History
- Old statues
- War mermorials
- Borrowed words from a rival culture
