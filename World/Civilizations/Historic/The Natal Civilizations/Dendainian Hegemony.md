# Pre-formation
For thousands of years, the elves lived off the land of the Predna's North-Western peninsula. These hunter-gatherers primarily lived in minor isolated enclaves, who primarily relied on hunting and gathering from the rich Fradon Jungle while smaller enclaves lived further into the peninsula.
# Instigation
Their long-lives and perfectionist nature meant that many of these enclaves remained extremely small with minimal reproduction all the while mastering artisanal crafts.
Around 5500 ya the Elves of North [[Frada]] began performing agricultural practices south of [[Lake Eradia]]. This sparked a migration out of the Fradon jungle and into central Frada with the enclaves forming into minor villages.![[Dendain Village.jpg]]
# Formation
As these highly skilled enclaves formed with their high quality crafts they began to interact. Many engaged in simple trade but before long minor spats began. In time each enclave selected a Den, an elder specialized in their craft would meet with one another to discuss resolutions.
