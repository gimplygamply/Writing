## Fragments of Etyo
The flayed and mangled pieces of the Aspect [[Etyo]], strewn across the universe by [[Aurel, The Comet of Divine Indomination]] during revenge of her [[Stolen Legacy]].
They are intangible to mortals and require great power, typically unachievable by mortals, to harness. The deities are able to meaningfully interact with these shards but doing so is a violation of [[Etyan, Repertoire of Etyo]]'s laws and they will face his punishment.
### Shards of Etyo
The Shards of Etyo, Also Known as [[Laws Of Magic#Grails|Grails]], Are minor fragments of Etyo. Through the power of Etyan, these grails can nest themselves within mortals to act as a catalyst to manipulate the Etyo around them
