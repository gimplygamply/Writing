- ### Description
	- Ella Redstream is a 70 year old female human street vendor.
	- She has very long, straight, brown hair and gray eyes.
	- She has veiny golden skin.
	- She stands 144cm (4'8") tall and has a regular build.
	- She has a round, very typical face.
	- She is branded as a prisoner on her neck.
	- {She has a plain english accent}
- ### Traits
	- She discretely worships Cyric, God of murder, lies, intrigue, strife, deception, illusion. (Chaotic Evil)
	- She is very direct.
	- She is materialistic.
	- She despises the aristocracy.
	- She is haunted by horrible memories.
